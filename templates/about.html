About This Project

What is Crop Price Predictor?

Crop Price Predictor is an innovative tool designed to help farmers, traders, and agricultural stakeholders predict crop prices based on rainfall patterns and historical data.

How It Works

Our system analyzes historical crop price data along with rainfall information to identify patterns and relationships. Using advanced machine learning algorithms, we can predict how changes in rainfall might affect crop prices in the future.

Why Use Crop Price Predictor?

  • For Farmers: Plan your planting and harvesting strategies based on predicted prices.
  • For Traders: Make informed decisions about buying and selling agricultural commodities.
  • For Policymakers: Understand market trends to develop effective agricultural policies.
  • For Researchers: Access valuable data on the relationship between climate factors and crop prices.

Supported Crops

Our system currently supports price prediction for several major crops including Wheat, Barley, Cotton, Paddy, and Ragi. We're continuously working to expand our database to include more crops.
